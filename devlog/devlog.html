Initialization and Conceptualization

I have decided to develop my first game! This is exciting but very intimidating. I have never done anything like this before and I have no idea how to do it. The first logical step was to create a GitHub repository and Project, and lay out how the project folder will be organized. This is the layout I am starting with:

The concept of the game is extremely simple, it's a clicker game. You are a blacksmith in a fantasy style world. A war has broken out, destroying most of your village. Your shop survived and the general of the army has commissioned you to provide the soldiers with swords, which will be made with a click. The goal of the game is to make as many swords as you can. There will be upgrades available, such as hiring apprentices to automatically make swords, and merchants to sell them. To keep it simple, there will only be two items to personally keep track of. Swords and gold. Swords are what you make by clicking, and gold is what you get for selling them. This should hopefully allow upgrades for the sword's value to be increased, somewhat infinitely.

Art and Design

I'm going for a pixel art style for this game, and will be designing all the visual elements personally unless I require help. I am using the Asperite software to create the sprites. The first thing I think I should design is the anvil, which will be the item clicked to make swords. I think I'm going to make all assets on a 32x32 canvas.
